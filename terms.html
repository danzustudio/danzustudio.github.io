HOW WE WORK

DANZU offers a collaborative design process that ensures you get the perfect design for your needs. Here's what to expect:

Consultation: We'll start by getting to know your industry and specific design requirements.

Research: Our team will conduct thorough research to understand your niche market and identify design trends.

Sketching: We'll create a visual representation of your design concept and share it with you for transparent collaboration.

Feedback: You can provide feedback at this stage, with three revision levels available:
Minor Revisions: Small adjustments to the design.
Major Revisions: More significant changes to the design concept.
Macro Revisions: Overhaul of the design direction.

Delivery: Once the design is finalized and payment is complete, we'll deliver all the final design files to you.



TERMS & CONDITIONS

Project Initiation: A 50% deposit is required to begin the research phase of your project.

Revisions: Three rounds of revisions are included in the project fee. Additional revisions may require an additional fee based on the scope of the changes.

Delivery: The remaining 50% of the project fee is due upon final approval of the design and before delivery of the final files.

Communication: We value clear and consistent communication throughout the design process. To ensure a smooth workflow, we kindly request you to avoid any periods of unresponsiveness during the project.
